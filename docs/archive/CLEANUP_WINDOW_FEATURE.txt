## Current Capture Modes

Your application now has two reliable capture modes:

1. **📐 Select Region** - Draw custom region with mouse
   - Full control over capture area
   - Works across all monitors
   - Aspect ratio locking support
   
2. **🖥️ Full Screen** - Dropdown menu for monitor selection
   - Primary monitor option
   - Secondary monitor options

Both modes work reliably without the complexity of window tracking.
